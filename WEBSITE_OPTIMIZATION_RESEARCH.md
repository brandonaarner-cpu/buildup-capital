4. **Conversion Optimization** (reduce friction, guide action)

**Opportunity:** Elevate to premium-tier with strategic refinements

#### 4.2 Tiered Service Levels

**Create 3-Tier Borrower Experience:**

**Tier 1: First-Time Borrower**
- Standard underwriting (5-7 days)
- Full documentation required
- Standard pricing
- Relationship manager assigned after closing

**Tier 2: Repeat Borrower**
- Expedited underwriting (3-5 days)
- Reduced documentation (leverage prior deals)
- Preferred pricing (0.5% rate discount)
- Dedicated relationship manager
- Priority deal review

**Tier 3: Elite Partner** (5+ deals funded)
- 24-48 hour underwriting
- Streamlined documentation
- Best pricing (1% rate discount)
- Direct access to decision-makers
- Co-branding opportunities (if desired)
- Referral commission program
- First look at off-market capital sources

**Benefits:**
- Incentivizes repeat business
- Creates loyalty/stickiness
- Justifies rate premiums for Tier 1 (Tier 2-3 get discounts)
- Builds long-term relationships
