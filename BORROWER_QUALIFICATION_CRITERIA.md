# BuildUp Capital - Borrower Qualification Criteria
**Loan Range:** $250,000 - $10,000,000  
**Type:** Real estate-backed bridge loans

---

## Core Requirements (Must Have)

### 1. Collateral (Real Estate)
**Minimum:**
- Must own real estate to secure loan
- Property must have sufficient equity
- Property types: Commercial, residential rental, mixed-use, land with permits

**Key Metrics:**
- **LTV (Loan-to-Value):** Maximum 75% (loan ÷ current property value)
- **ARV (After Repair Value):** If renovation, maximum 65% LTV on ARV
- **Lien Position:** First position strongly preferred

**Questions to Ask:**
- What property will secure the loan?
- Current market value? (recent appraisal or estimate)
- Current mortgage balance? (to calculate equity)
- Property type and condition?
- Any other liens or encumbrances?

---

### 2. Loan Purpose (One of These)

**A. Business Acquisition**
- Acquiring existing operating business
- Acquiring business + real estate
- Management buyout
- Franchise purchase

**B. Cash Flow Business (Operating)**
- Working capital for existing business
- Equipment purchase
- Expansion capital
- Debt consolidation

**C. Real Estate Investment**
- Property acquisition (buy-and-hold or flip)
- Property renovation/improvement
- Construction/development (with permits)
- Portfolio expansion

**Questions to Ask:**
- What will the funds be used for?
- If acquisition: What are you buying? (business, property, both)
- If existing business: What industry? How long operating?
- Timeline for use of funds?

---

### 3. Cash Flow & Ability to Repay

**For Operating Business:**
- **DSCR (Debt Service Coverage Ratio):** Minimum 1.15x
  - Formula: (Net Operating Income) ÷ (Annual Debt Payments)
  - Example: $150K NOI ÷ $100K debt service = 1.5x ✅

**For Business Acquisition:**
- Business must generate sufficient cash flow to cover debt
- Seller's financials required (last 2 years P&L)
- Buyer must demonstrate industry experience OR strong business plan

**For Real Estate Investment:**
- **Exit Strategy Required:**
  - Sale within 6-24 months (fix-and-flip)
  - Refinance to conventional loan (stabilization)
  - SBA loan (business purchase)
  - Cash-out from separate transaction

**Questions to Ask:**
- Current business revenue? (if operating business)
- Net income or cash flow? (monthly/annual)
- How will you repay the loan? (cash flow, sale, refinance)
- Timeline for exit?

---

### 4. Personal Financial Strength

**Credit:**
- Minimum 620 credit score (640+ preferred)
- No recent bankruptcies (within 3 years)
- No current foreclosures or liens

**Liquidity:**
- Cash reserves: Minimum 3-6 months of debt payments
- Example: $10K/month payment = $30K-60K cash reserves

**Net Worth:**
- Minimum net worth: Equal to loan amount
- Example: $500K loan = $500K net worth required

**Questions to Ask:**
- Approximate credit score?
- Cash on hand? (liquid reserves)
- Total assets vs. liabilities? (rough net worth)
- Any bankruptcies, foreclosures, or major issues?

---

## Red Flags (Likely Decline)

❌ **No collateral** (unsecured loan request)  
❌ **LTV >80%** (insufficient equity)  
❌ **No exit strategy** (how will they repay?)  
❌ **Credit score <580** (high risk)  
❌ **Recent bankruptcy** (<3 years)  
❌ **No business experience** (if acquiring business)  
❌ **Negative cash flow** (business losing money)  
❌ **Property in foreclosure** or legal issues  
❌ **Unclear loan purpose** (red flag for fraud)

## Qualification Funnel Logic

### Tier 1: Auto-Approve (Fast Track)
- Loan $250K-$2M
- LTV <65%
- Credit score 680+
- Operating business with 1.5x+ DSCR
- Clear exit strategy
**Action:** Schedule call within 24 hours

### Tier 2: Standard Review
- Loan $250K-$5M
- LTV 65-75%
- Credit score 620-680
- Business with 1.15-1.5x DSCR OR clear exit plan
**Action:** Schedule call within 48 hours

### Tier 3: Manual Underwriting
- Loan $5M-$10M
- LTV >75% (but strong compensating factors)
- Credit score 580-620 (but strong business/assets)
- Complex deal structure
**Action:** Detailed financial review before call

### Tier 4: Likely Decline
- LTV >80%
- Credit <580
- No collateral or exit strategy
- Red flags present
**Action:** Polite decline email OR request additional info
